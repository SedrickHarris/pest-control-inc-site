## Required Page Structure
1. H1 with service + neighborhood + city
2. Direct answer paragraph
3. Neighborhood pest context (desert adjacency, age of homes, etc.)
4. Services available in this neighborhood
5. Internal links: parent city page, /pest-control-las-vegas/ or parent city hub,
   /free-estimate/, 2-3 species pages
6. FAQ (minimum 3, neighborhood-localized)
7. CTA section

## Publishing Rule
Do not publish thin neighborhood pages. Every page must have unique:
- H1, meta title, meta description
- Local intro paragraph
- Minimum 3 FAQs

## No-Fake-Data Rules
- Do not invent neighborhood demographics or crime statistics
- Do not claim specific response times per neighborhood
- Do not invent neighborhood-specific reviews
