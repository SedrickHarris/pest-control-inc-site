What the Health Conscious Service Program Means in Practice

The Health Conscious Service Program is the operational expression of every value we hold:

🐾

Because our mission is focused on pet safety and family safety, our Health Conscious Service Program uses EPA-registered low-impact baits and gels rather than broad chemical sprays. Because our mission is focused on environmental responsibility, our eco-conscious pest control services use non-toxic, low-impact, minimal-impact formulations focused on the exterior of your property first. Environmentally responsible pest control means treating only where pests are, with only what is needed.

Our value of excellence → Our method of IPM inspection before treatment

Because our mission is focused on customer satisfaction through results — not just activity — our Integrated Pest Management approach inspects and identifies before treating. We treat the source of pest activity, not just the visible symptoms. This produces better long-term results and less chemical exposure for Las Vegas families.

🤝
Our value of integrity → Our method of transparent service reporting

Because our value of integrity demands accountability, every Pest Control Inc service visit includes a written report of what was found, what was treated, and what we recommend. No hidden information. No hidden fees. Mission driven service means you know exactly what was done and why.
