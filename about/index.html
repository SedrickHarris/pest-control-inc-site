Our Method: The Health Conscious Service Program

The Health Conscious Service Program is Pest Control Inc's proprietary approach to pest management — built on the principles of Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and refined across three generations of family service in Las Vegas. It is the reason our treatments are effective against Nevada's toughest pests while remaining safe for your family, your pets, and the Las Vegas desert environment.

What Is Integrated Pest Management (IPM)?

Integrated Pest Management is a science-based approach to pest control that combines biological, physical, cultural, and targeted chemical methods to manage pest populations with the least possible impact on people, pets, and the environment. Rather than defaulting to broad chemical application, IPM identifies the root cause of pest activity and addresses it at the source — treating the condition, not just the symptom.

IPM is the standard endorsed by the National Pest Management Association and the regulatory framework recognized by the Nevada Department of Agriculture — the two authorities that govern professional pest control in our state.

How PCI's Health Conscious Service Program Works

1

Inspect

Every service begins with a thorough inspection of your Las Vegas property — interior and exterior. Our licensed technician identifies active pest populations, entry points, conducive conditions, and risk areas specific to your property type and neighborhood.

2

Identify

We identify the specific pest species, infestation severity, and contributing environmental factors. Las Vegas pest pressure is highly localized — scorpion activity in Summerlin differs from rodent pressure in North Las Vegas. Identification drives the treatment plan.

3

Treat

We apply targeted, low-impact EPA-registered treatments — baits, gels, and pinpoint applications — focused on the exterior of your property first. Interior treatments applied only where needed, using products appropriate for family and pet safe environments. No broad chemical sprays.

4

Monitor & Prevent

We seal entry points, remove conducive conditions, and apply residual barriers that protect your property between visits. On monthly and quarterly plans, we monitor results on every return visit and adjust the treatment approach based on what we find.

Why IPM Instead of Traditional Pest Control?

Traditional pest control applies chemical treatments on a fixed schedule regardless of actual pest activity. IPM applies targeted treatments based on real inspection data — which means less chemical exposure for your family and more effective long-term results for your Las Vegas property. That is the Pest Control Inc difference.
