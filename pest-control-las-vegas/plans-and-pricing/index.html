WHY RECURRING PEST CONTROL · LAS VEGAS DESERT CLIMATE

Why Las Vegas Homes Need Year-Round Pest Protection

The Mojave Desert doesn't have pest seasons — it has pest cycles. Scorching summer heat drives bark scorpions, cockroaches, and ants indoors. Winter brings rodent pressure through foundation gaps. Monsoon moisture in July and August activates termites, silverfish, and fly populations. A one-time treatment eliminates the current cycle. A monthly or quarterly pest protection plan prevents the next one before it starts.

🏜️
Desert Never Sleeps

Las Vegas pests are active 10–12 months per year. Monthly and quarterly plans maintain a continuous barrier so infestations never get a chance to establish.

🔄
Free Re-Services Included

Plan members receive free re-service calls between scheduled visits. One-time treatment customers pay full price for every follow-up. The plan pays for itself in the first callback.

☀️
Barrier Compounds Break Down

Exterior treatment barriers degrade in 30–90 days under Las Vegas UV and extreme heat. Regular visits maintain a live barrier — not just a memory of one.
